How to Get 5-Star Reviews with Professional Cleaning

📅 March 2026 By RH Cleaning Solutions LTD 6 min read

For AirBnB hosts, cleanliness is one of the biggest factors influencing guest satisfaction. Many five-star reviews specifically mention how clean and welcoming a property felt upon arrival. Investing in professional cleaning can significantly increase positive feedback and encourage repeat bookings.

A professionally cleaned AirBnB bedroom ready for guests in North Wales

What Guests Really Notice

Guests expect spotless kitchens, sparkling bathrooms, fresh bedding, and dust-free living spaces. Even small details—such as fingerprints on mirrors, crumbs under furniture, or unpleasant odours—can affect ratings.

Essential Pre-Guest Checklist

  • Freshly washed linens and neatly folded towels
  • Emptied bins and replenished toiletries
  • Kitchen appliances cleaned inside and out
  • Floors thoroughly vacuumed and mopped
  • Dust-free surfaces and cobweb-free corners

The Professional Advantage

Professional cleaners also inspect the property for overlooked issues like cobwebs, stains, or maintenance concerns that could negatively impact the guest experience.

Our team performing a quick-turnover clean for AirBnB hosts

Finishing Touches That Impress

Adding thoughtful finishing touches, such as neatly arranged cushions, fresh flowers, or a welcome note, can leave a lasting impression.

Maintaining consistent cleaning standards after every booking helps build trust with guests and improves your property's ranking on booking platforms.
