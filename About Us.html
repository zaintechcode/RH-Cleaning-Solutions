About Us

Cleaning Services in North Wales

RH Cleaning Solutions LTD is a trusted, fully registered cleaning company based in Beddgelert, North Wales, providing high-quality residential and commercial cleaning services in Bangor and the surrounding areas. We are committed to delivering reliable, professional, and eco-friendly cleaning solutions tailored to the needs of every client.

Whether you need regular house cleaning, office cleaning, Airbnb changeovers, hospitality cleaning, or a thorough deep clean, our experienced team delivers exceptional results with meticulous attention to detail and the highest standards of cleanliness.

At RH Cleaning Solutions LTD, customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We combine dependable service, flexible scheduling, and competitive pricing to ensure every property is left spotless, refreshed, and ready to enjoy.
